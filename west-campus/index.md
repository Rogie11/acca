<h2 class="wp-block-heading">About</h2>



<p>ACCA West Campus is located at Cobb County . It has about 150 students. At this campus, we offer two Chinese immersion learning tracks, using JiNan and Ma Liping textbooks. Grade levels range from K to 10. For students without or with limited Chinese language background, we offer Bilingual Chinese classes from Level 1 to 6, which are all taught in English. In addition, we offer various enrichment classes, such as programming, Children’s painting, Chinese painting, Go game, Chess, Children’s vocal, SAT reading/writing, public speaking, and adult yoga. All classes are offered on Sunday afternoon. 少数班级在西校区开设( hybrid) 实体+ 网课同步课堂.</p>



<p>Campus Location:<br>Hightower Trail Middle School, 3905 Post Oak Tritt Rd, Marietta, 30062</p>
