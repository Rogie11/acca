<h2 class="wp-block-heading">About</h2>



<p>ACCA South Campus is located at prestigious Emory University. It has about 200 students. At this campus, we offer two Chinese immersion learning tracks, using JiNan and Ma Liping textbooks. Grade levels range from K to 10. For students without or with limited Chinese language background, we offer Bilingual Chinese classes from Level 1 to 6, which are all taught in English. In addition, we offer various enrichment classes, such as programming, Children’s painting, Chinese painting, Go game, Chess, Children’s vocal, SAT reading/writing, public speaking, and adult yoga. All classes are offered on Sunday afternoon. For registration details, please contact <a href="mailto:south.admin@acca-web.org">south.admin@acca-web.org</a>.</p>



<p>Address: <br>Emory University, 537 Kilgo Circle, Atlanta, GA 30322</p>
