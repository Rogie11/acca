<p>North Saturday campus is the largest campus of ACCA, with nearly 700 students enrolled in 2023. The campus was founded in 1999. We teach Pre-k to 10<sup>th</sup> grade and AP Chinese class. By using multiple classic textbooks, including Jinan Chinese, Ma Li Ping Chinese, Chinese Made easy for bilingual Chinese provided to kids with different various Chinese-learning requirements at ages 4-18yrs old. To the convenience of the families who live near the location of North Saturday Campus, we also provide a variety of enrichment classes for kids with additional requirements. Including Children’s painting, Youth Comics, Chinese chess, International chess, Scratch, Python, SAT reading/writing, etc.</p>
