<p><strong>The President’s Volunteer Service Award is on a temporary pause, as decided by AmeriCorps effective May 27, 2025.</strong></p>



<p>亚特兰大现代中文学校(ACCA）已被授权成为可以为志愿者验证义工服务时间并颁发美国“总统义工奖”&nbsp;的机构。</p>



<p>总统义工奖（The President&#8217;s Volunteer Service Award）是全美最高荣誉的义工服务奖，是为鼓励美国居民，特别是年轻一代踊跃为社区义务服务，共同创造美好的学习、工作和生活环境而设的奖项。更多详情请查阅 <a href="https://presidentialserviceawards.gov/" data-type="URL" data-id="https://presidentialserviceawards.gov/" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer noopener">https://presidentialserviceawards.gov/</a></p>
